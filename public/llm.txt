# Local Surge SEO

> High-impact, hyper-targeted Local SEO and Search Signal Diagnostics for businesses in the US and Canada.

Local Surge SEO is a professional marketing intelligence platform providing automated local visibility audits, schema markups, geo-targeted rank analysis, and customizable lead retention workspaces.

## Core Capabilities & Services

- **Free SEO Diagnostic Auditor:** Real-time diagnostics parsing local citation profile signals, Google Business Profile validation status, responsive page load factors, and targeted search signals in specific ZIP codes.
- **State & City SEO Directories:** Dedicated search signals optimized for specific regions, centering major state corridors (such as California) and city hubs (such as Los Angeles).
- **Automated Schema Markup Generator:** Builds JSON-LD structured schemas compliant with Google's rich result guidelines to declare LocalBusiness identities, rating aggregates, and business locations.
- **Local Directory Auditing Tool:** Cross-compares local physical details (NAP: Name, Address, Phone) across major directory providers.
- **Active Subscription Models:** Custom rolling, contract-free monthly SEO signal booster plans designed to build high-authority backlinks, fix citation consistency gaps, and track organic placement patterns.
- **Dynamic Leader Boards:** Track the highest-profile local outreach performance and live keyword rankings.
